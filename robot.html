Paly Robotics

Award Winning FRC Robot Designing and Fabrication

  • As a member of Team 8 Paly Robotics for four years in high school, I was heavily involved in designing and fabricating robots which won numerous awards for their designs and performances.
  • In 2016, we qualified to the World Championships for the first time in the team's history in over five years, and in 2017, we reached the semifinals.
  • We fabricated our robots in our lab using machines such as CNC mills and routers, laser cutter, tig welder, and lathe.
